The Pill Man

This is The Pill Man. I stumbled upon a wierd sticker at school that I frequently passed by when going to class. It was this wierd figure in a suit that had this strange head. To me it looked like a pill, but after asking someone else, they thought it resembled a woman's finger with the nail painted red. Both were really cool to me but I couldn't get this Pill Man idea out of my head so I threw it into Illustrator. I'm not sure what it represents. I think it represnts whatever you want it to. To me, it feels very "big brother" or "obey giant". Anyways, I hope you enjoy this ominous figure. You can buy stickers at Redbubble.com
