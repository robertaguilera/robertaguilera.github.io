Trayvon Bromell

This was a birthday gift which turned into a really awesome motivational graphic. Knowing Trayvon Bromell's story, I wanted to add a phrase or saying that I felt really spoke to his journey and this is what I came up with. The reference photo used was actually of him in a Baylor uniform. I had to swap that for a USA uniform because I wanted to show how very far he has come. I also added some brush strokes behind him to give it that "Olympics" feel. Check it out on Redbubble.com.
