Concepts

This was not an actual rebranding for Rochester Refugee Resettlement Service. It was just a personal project that I did since their current logo is very outdated. In my concepts I did alot of globes and people holding hands. I wanted to get a welcoming feel across and I think the colors help with that.

Final Logo

The final design can be used with or without the text, depending on the application. In the end I decided to shed one side of the winding road and just keep that one line for reference. Check out their current logo here
