Thoughtful Sound

The logo for Thoughtful Sound was very simple. The only requirement was that it was a cartoon looking grammy award. I drew it in Illustrator and took a stylistic approach to some of the shading, then I applied the gold/metal texture that you see on it now. Check out the logo on the website.
