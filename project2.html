SMIF

A food management device that uses deep learning to keep inventory, suggest recipes, and remind you when you're running low on groceries. This idea was featured at RIT's annual Imagine RIT where all kinds of projects and ideas can be displayed, presented and viewed by the Rochester public. Our booth had one of the highest visitor counts for our category of Health.
