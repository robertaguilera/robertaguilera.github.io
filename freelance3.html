Redrawing

For this project I simplified and reillustrated hand exercises that paitents would be given by their therapist. The previous packet was very outdated and was made up of hand drawings that were scanned and printed. I tried to make these new illustrations as clear as possible so that it would be easier for an older demographic to understand.

Final Layout

Once I had finished the drawings, I started to work on a new layout for the pages. The pages are split into the categories of exercises and I left space for a date and potential comments by the therapist. This is an example of one of the pages.
