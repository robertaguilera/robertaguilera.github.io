Cascata

Cascata is an elegant new solution to your boring napkin dilemma. It houses the salt and pepper shakers directly, saving space. It also breaks out of the boxiness of typical napkin holders that are on the market. The napkins spill over the edge and dispense at an angle, to give it a more dynamic look (this also makes the napkins easier to grab). Napkins are tucked in and under the rounded top piece, giving them some integrity.
