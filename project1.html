Top Gauge

A redesign of the commonly used Tire Pressure Gauge worked out from initial research to a finished digital 3D model. We developed the Top Gauge. A visually elegant, yet functional, pressure reading tool. It's excess surface area makes for a more comfortable grip compared to the typical pen gauge.
