AmbiLux

AmbiLux is a dual purpose lamp designed to adress your storage needs. The S-shape provides two shelves for devices or other materials and the top panel rotates on a hinge to serve as a desk light or normal bedside ambient light. The Oak and Maple wood frame in complimented by some upcycled circuit boards which I purchased on Etsy.
