Victim of Society

This is cover art I designed and painted for a completely made up album I chose to title Victim of Society. It is a portrait of TUT, a rapper from Chattanooga, Tennessee. His Debut album Preacher's Son had a similar digitally painted cover art. I was also very inspired by Sam Spratt's work and the celebrity portraits he does.
