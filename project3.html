The adjustable bracelet allows the user to change volume and upvote/downvote a music selection all from their wrist.

The bracelet can stand alone but the speaker offers a wireless charging dock and integrated light system.

MoodRing

A smart wearable bracelet that collects various data such as heartrate, body temperature and motion tracking to play music accordingly. Moodring plays your personal music from a streaming service of choice, or from your own music library depending on how it is set up. Moodring also includes a speaker paired via bluetooth and glows a certain color according to the data collected, hence its name.
